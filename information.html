Digits Recognition
AI Information

The AI model :

I created an ia model from scratch in Python. With only libraries like Numpy or maptplotlib. In the absence of a learning library, all calculations have to be redone. I trained my model on this MNIST dataset, giving it 40,000 28-by-28-pixel photos of handwritten digits. On the train set, my model achieves an accuracy of around 85%. (With a possible risk of overfitting).

AI integration :

Using the javascript programming language, I created a canvas that can be drawn, then at the end of a plot javascript will resize the canvas in the background to a size of 28 by 28 pixels. Then, using the mathjs library, I give the canvas image to my model, which then gives me the number with the highest probability.

AI error field:

Training ia is not perfect without the use of training libraries or just by overfitting, but also the fact that the canva is resized increases the potential for error.
